General Information

Welcome to RECOMB 2014, the 18th Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology!

RECOMB 2014 will take place in the Oakland neighborhood of Pittsburgh, home to Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Pittsburgh, from April 2-5, 2014. The conference aims to bridge the computational, mathematical, statistical, and biological sciences. The conference features keynote talks by preeminent scientists in life sciences, proceeding presentations of peer-reviewed research papers in computational biology, and poster sessions on the latest research progress. As usual, authors of accepted papers will be invited to submit an edited version of the manuscript to the Journal of Computational Biology for the publication of a special issue.
