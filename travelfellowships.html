TRAVEL FELLOWSHIPS

ISCB travel fellowship

Thanks to generous support from the International Society for Computational Biology (ISCB), we have roughly 5,000 USD available to fund about 10 attendees. These awards are only available to ISCB student members. To apply, please

  • Download and fill out this ISCB travel fellowship application form.
  • Send the filled and signed form as a single pdf file to recomb-admin@compbio.cmu.edu, with "RECOMB 2014 --- ISCB Travel Fellowship Application" as the subject of your email.

    NSF travel fellowship

    Thanks to generous support from the US National Science Foundation (NSF), we have roughly 12,000 USD available to fund up to 12 attendees. These awards are only available to students in US institutions. To apply, please

  • Download and fill out this NSF travel fellowship application form.
  • Send the filled and signed form as a single pdf file to recomb-admin@compbio.cmu.edu, with "RECOMB 2014 --- NSF Travel Fellowship Application" as the subject of your email.

    All applicants should have completed the online registration before the application. Due to the limited amount of travel fellowships, each person can apply for only one travel fellowship. Applications should be submitted no later than March 1, 2014. You should receive an email acknowledging the receipt of your application within 3 business. If not, please resubmit. The result will be announced to each applicant by email around March 10, 2014.
