18th Annual International Conference on Research in Computational Molecular Biology
Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
April 2-5, 2014

Call for Posters

RECOMB 2014 invites the submission of abstracts for the poster track.

Please submit a 250-word abstract of your poster that describes its primary results. Your submission should also include the author(s), their affiliations, the e-mail address of at least the contact author, and the poster title as requested by the submission system. The abstract should be plain text and not contain figures. Accepted poster abstracts will be published as submitted in the conference book of abstracts.

Submit your poster abstract through the EasyChair system at: https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=recomb2014.

POSTER GUIDELINES

  • Each poster should fit within a 120cm wide x 120cm high (48in x 48in) space.
  • The poster must cover the same material as the accepted abstract.
  • At least one author of the poster must register and pay to attend the conference and present an accepted poster.
  • The paper title and authors' names should be placed prominently at the top of the poster to allow viewers to identify your poster easily.
  • It is recommended that the text and illustrations should be readable from at least two meters (six feet) away.
  • Posters should be set up according to the assigned schedule which will be shown in the program book. Tacks, adhesives and materials will be provided for setting up your posters.

    KEY DATES

    Submission deadline: February 10, 2014*
    Acceptance notification: February 24, 2014

    *Additional posters may be accepted as space permits until the early registration deadline.
